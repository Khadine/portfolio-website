About the Designer

Hi, my name is Khadine Caines, I'm an Artist and Designer based in Los Angeles, CA. I have a background in Fine Arts with a specialization in Printmaking. As a printmaker, it is important to understand the impact of a simple design. This understanding has helped me become a successful UI Designer.

Having 12 years of experience as a practicing Artist, I am able to utilize my deep understanding of conceptual design, color theory and visual design to better inform my user interface designs development. With an expertise in visual design, I am able to focus on user centric solutions, bringing concepts to reality.

Alongside my BFA, I have completed the CareerFoundry UI Design Course, making me well-versed in app development, wire framing and prototyping.

With this specialized skill set, I am able to create intuitive and beautiful UI Designs.
